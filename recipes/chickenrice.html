Chicken and rice

This is a quick air fryer chicken and rice recipe

Ingredients list:

  • 4oz chicken breast (271 calories)
  • Half cup of dry minute rice (Makes 1 cup cooked!) (110 calories)
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil (119 calories)

Prepping and Cooking instructions:

  1. Preheat the air fryer using the preheat option
  2. Coat your chicken with olive oil and the seasoning of your choice
  3. Put half a cup of water in a bowl and add the dry rice
  4. Microvwave it for about 3 minutes
  5. When the air fryer is ready, pop your chicken breast in
  6. Use then chicken setting on the air fryer
  7. Serve and enjoy!
