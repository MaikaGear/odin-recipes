Grilled cheese

This is a quick and easy, yet fun and delicious recipe you can prep and cook in under 30 minutes. It is perfect for workers on the go or a mother with many mouths to feed and even just someone who wants to enjoy an All-american breakfast wihtout spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients list:

  • 2 slices Artesano bread (220 calories)
  • Cheese of your choice
  • 1 tbsp butter (102 calories)

Prepping and Cooking instructions:

  1. Melt the butter in a pan
  2. Add both slices of bread and cook 1 side on both slices until golden brown
  3. Flip one slice and quickly add the cheese on the slice you flipped
  4. Flip the other slice onto the other bread and finish cooking the last sides

  7. While the pan is hot, put the bread into the pan and let it cook
  8. When the bread is golden brown, remove it
  9. Enjoy!
